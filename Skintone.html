Skintone and Body measurement based Costume Recommendation

github

Blendaura tailors fashion recommendations to your skin tone and body measurements.

Blendaura is a cutting-edge fashion recommendation system that personalizes costume suggestions based on users' skin tone and body measurements. This project is designed to enhance the shopping experience by providing tailored recommendations that align with individual characteristics.

Blendaura utilizes a Python Flask framework to build a robust and user-friendly web application. The system collects and processes user data, including skin parameters such as melanin levels, erythema, and skin color, along with body measurements. This data is crucial for generating accurate and personalized recommendations.
